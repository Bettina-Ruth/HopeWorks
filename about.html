About HopeWorks

HopeWorks connects job seekers, hirers, and NGOs through a practical local hiring platform. The project is designed to reduce friction: browsing stays open, while actions that affect real accounts or job records are protected behind login.

The latest update adds role-based registration, temporary in-memory login, conditional navigation, protected actions, and a profile area with a signature pad.
